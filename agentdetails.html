About

I am multi-lingual Agent can speak English, Chinese, Hindi, Punjabi and Urdu. I was born and raiseed in India, I moved to Canada to further my academic carrer and pursue a path in Hospitality and Culinary; I have always been fascinated by real estate industry and so when I decided that real estate was the right career for me, I jumped in with both feet and never looked back; I believe that real estate is about helping people. The home buying process can be frustrating and confusing, especially for first time buyers, but I take pride in my clients feel comfortable at all stages of the process. With constant communication and the highest commitmentof service, I am known for my authenticity, timely responses and enthusiasm; When I am not helping families buy/sell homes, I enjoy table tennins, movies, anything technology related and spending time with my loved ones and my 2 Yorkies Cheeku and Kiwi.
