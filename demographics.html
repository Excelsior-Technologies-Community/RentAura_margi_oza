There are 2,794,355 residents in Toronto, with an average age of 41.5 Males make up 48.3% of the population, while femalesaccount for 51.7%. Locals 25 to 34 years old represent the largest age group in Toronto (490,740 individuals), followed by those aged over 65 (476,985 people).
