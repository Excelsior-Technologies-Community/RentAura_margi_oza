About Us

Mexico Property Search is an online real estate marketplace with more than 20 years of experience in the housing industry, featuring residential and commercial listings.

Over 20+ years experience in real estate services

Whether you're interested in buying a home, seeking apartments for rent, searching for office space or looking to invest in commercial properties, Mexico Property Search is the place to start. Plus, if you're a real estate agent, we can help boost your business by efficiently advertising your properties on our platform. That's because Mexico Property Search generates more than 90 million visits per year and is present on multiple markets. Headquartered in Saskatoon, we operate in Canada, the United States and several international locations, including Mexico, puerto Rico, Costa Rica, the Dominican Republic and more.

Our History

Mexixo Property Search's journey began in 1996, evolving from agent tools to a full-service search portal known for market expertise and wide listings.

1996

Mexico Property Search was initially founded in 1996 to provide agent websites and CRM solutions to real estate professionals. Eventually, the company transitioned to a full-service real estate search portal. Throughout the years, Mexico Property Search made a name for itself through its market expertise and extended listing distribution services, in addition to establishing partnerships with numerous North American MLSs. The platform was primarily focused on the homebuying niche.
